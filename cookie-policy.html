Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 2026

By using the Blood Bank Management System, you accept the use of cookies and browser storage as described below.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help the system remember information about your session and preferences.

How We Use Cookies

  • Maintain login sessions and authentication status
  • Improve user experience and navigation
  • Store basic preferences and settings

Local Storage

The system may also use browser local storage to save user preferences, remember selections, and improve overall functionality.

Data Privacy

We do not use cookies to store sensitive personal data. Any stored data is used to support the application experience.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not work correctly if cookies are turned off.

Changes to This Policy

This cookie policy may be updated in the future to reflect changes in system behavior.
