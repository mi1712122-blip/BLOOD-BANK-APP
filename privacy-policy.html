Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026

Welcome to the Blood Bank Management System.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Blood Group
  • City/Location
  • Phone Number
  • Profile Image (optional)

Why We Collect Information

The information is collected to:

  • Connect blood donors with hospitals and organizations
  • Manage blood requests and donations
  • Improve user experience and system functionality
  • Maintain secure user accounts

Data Storage

User data is securely stored using Firebase services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firestore Database
  • Firebase Storage

Data Protection

We do not sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties.

Reasonable security measures are used to protect user information.

User Rights

Users may update or remove their account information at any time.

Cookies and Local Storage

The system may use browser storage or cookies to maintain login sessions and improve functionality.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future if the system features change.

Contact

For any questions regarding this policy, please contact the system administrator.
